3. The Website Redesign: Translating the Physical Space
Currently, bevelandgold.com operates like a legal waiver. The homepage is dominated by strict shop policies (ID rules, late fees, age limits). While necessary, hitting cold traffic with a "wall of NOs" destroys conversion rates.

We are going to digitize your physical shop so the website feels exactly like walking through your front doors.

The Digital Color Palette: We extracted the exact tones of your interior. The site will utilize your Matte Charcoal walls (#1C1C1E) as the foundation, accented by the Olive/Sage Green (#616A53) of your accent wall, and highlighted with the Antiqued Gold (#D4AF37) of your logo and ornate mirror for our primary "Book Now" buttons.

Texture & Typography: We will use sophisticated Serif fonts (like Playfair Display) to match your window decals, and incorporate subtle digital textures—like arch-shaped image masks that mirror your antique floor mirror, and subtle vertical fluting to match your front desk.

The "Wall of Text" Fix: We will move all policies to a dedicated FAQ page and integrate a mandatory "I agree to these terms" checkbox into the booking widget. The homepage will be reserved exclusively for high-res jewelry loops and selling the luxury experience.

4. The Traffic Ecosystem & "Wall of Love"
Your current digital assets are operating on isolated islands. We are going to connect them into an automated revenue engine.

Fixing the TikTok Funnel: You have great traction on TikTok (@bevel.gold), but no bridge to your website. We will optimize your TikTok for Local SEO (Gen-Z uses TikTok like Google Maps) and build a dedicated /links landing page on the new site specifically designed to convert mobile social traffic instantly.

Every time a client leaves a 5-star review with a photo of their fresh piercing, the website will automatically update to display it, building 24/7 automated trust.

Bulletproof ADA Compliance: Search engines heavily reward sites that are built correctly. We will ensure the new site adheres strictly to WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards, ensuring proper contrast ratios and screen-reader compatibility. This ensures your site is legally protected and accessible to every potential client.
