Shop Policies

Please review our policies before booking an appointment to ensure a seamless experience.

A valid driver's license, state-issued photo ID, or passport must be provided for all piercings and services for clients over the age of 18.

No ID, no exceptions. Digital copies or pictures of an ID are not accepted.

Age requirements are strictly enforced and built directly into our booking system—if an option is not available for a minor, that service is not offered.

  • Children’s earlobe piercings begin at age 8, provided they show all signs of readiness.
  • We do not offer infant ear lobe piercings.

For the safety and integrity of your piercings, we require that all jewelry be purchased through our studio. We do not install outside jewelry, whether for new or existing piercings.

Additionally, downsizing to a shorter post is not included with the initial piercing and will require a separate transaction and appointment.

Not every anatomy is suited for every piercing. If we believe a placement will not heal properly, we may decline the request but will gladly discuss alternative options to ensure a safe and beautiful result.

Services we do NOT offer: surface anchors (dermal), surface bars, oral (tongue, smiley), cheek, male or female genitalia (if it requires you to remove clothing, we do not offer it).
